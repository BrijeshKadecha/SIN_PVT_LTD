Our people

People are at the heart of Gordon Mitchell Contractors – both the people who have entrusted us with building their homes, and our highly dedicated and skilled team who are turning their vision into reality.

Each and every member of our staff shares our ethos and takes pride in their work. Our Facility

GMC Purpose-Built Facility

Like our company, our purpose-built yard at Haugh of Sluie in Banchory, has been 30 years in the making. It now encompasses:

  • A large office development with sufficient parking
  • A timber systems factory
  • In-house joinery and workshop facilities
  • A heavy plant mechanical workshop
  • Multiple rental units for external companies
  • Vast storage space for materials and machinery
  • Loading zones for logistics
